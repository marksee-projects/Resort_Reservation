About the Resort

A Peaceful Escape for
Rest and Relaxation

Located in Brgy. Tagumpay, Orani, Bataan. Our resort offers an escape unlike any other. Wake up to the gentle noise of nature, dine at terrace area with a garden view, and let the warm Philippine sun restore your spirit.

Whether you're planning a romantic retreat, a fun-filled family vacation, or a productive corporate gathering, the resort provides a perfect blend of comfort, hospitality, and natural beauty along one of Bataan known resort.

  • Adult Pool & kiddie Pool
  • Events & Conference facilities
  • 24hour Security & Basketball Court
  • Parking Space & Common BBQ Grill Area
